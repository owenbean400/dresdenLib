Summer Reading 2011

Don't forget to sign your kids up this month for the Summer Reading program! This year our theme is One World, Many Stories, so we'll be traveling around the world, at least in our imaginations! The program is open to children age 3-11 and offers chances for readers and pre-readers to win prizes and have fun! Free Sea Dogs ticket vouchers will be offered again this year and extra prizes for every ten Box Tops for Education that are turned in! The start date for registration will be Saturday June 18th. Parents are strongly encouraged to sign their children up as early as possible so they have time to meet their summer reading goals. Please feel free to call 737-810 or drop in during library hours for more information about the program.

For more information call the library at 737-8810
