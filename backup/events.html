The Littlest Tugboat

October 21st, 10:00am - By Sarah Sherman McGrail -- The Littlest Tugboat is a cheerful children's book about a family of tugboats that works hard to guide ships up and down the Kennebec River. Tommy, the littlest Tugboat, chafes when his mother tells him to stay home during stormy weather, while his bigger siblings are hard at work. When Tommy ventures out on his own, he discovers a barge carried toward a low bridge by the storm--only the littlest tugboat can save the day, and the bridge, by guiding the barge to safety!

Please join us for a reading and book signing of this wonderful local children’s tale by a Southport Island native. After the reading the author also will explain how she got the idea for the story and how the book and illustrations come together to make a book!
